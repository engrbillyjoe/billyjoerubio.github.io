Relevant Projects

500kV/230kV Marilao EHV Substation

Participated in the configuration and commissioning of the Extra High Voltage (EHV) substation. Responsibilities included relay setting verification, secondary circuit wiring validation, and end-to-end SCADA integration with NGCP’s control center. Ensured correct data acquisition, signal mapping, and interlocking functionality for system safety and reliability.

Laoag 1 & 2 130MW Solar Power Plant

Led SCADA and relay protection configuration for two large-scale solar farms. Conducted communication setup via IEC 60870-5-104 and IEC 61850 protocols. Implemented and validated device mapping, real-time monitoring, and remote control commands for accurate performance data and reliable power dispatch.

San Jose & Cordon Solar Projects

Supported testing and configuration of NDME (Network Disturbance Monitoring Equipment) and protection relays for grid compliance. Performed relay coordination checks, data point verification, and testing of breaker control logic to ensure seamless integration with NGCP’s supervisory systems.

Tiwi Binary Geothermal Power Plant & Tiwi C NGCP Substation

Commissioned SCADA and protection systems for the Tiwi Binary Geothermal facility and its interconnection with the NGCP Tiwi C Substation. Configured relay communication, signal mapping, and control logic for generator synchronization and voltage regulation. Ensured safe and efficient coordination between geothermal units and the grid.

Taft Solar Power Project

Handled HV and PV SCADA configuration, protection relay setup, and PPC (Power Plant Controller) integration. Configured communication interfaces between the PPC, inverters, and grid controller to enable real-time monitoring, active/reactive power control, and automatic voltage regulation. Verified PPC logic to ensure proper synchronization and grid stability.

Power Plant Control (PPC) System Configuration

Designed and implemented AGC (Automatic Generation Control) and AVC (Automatic Voltage Control) logic within PPC systems for multiple solar and hybrid power plants. Ensured the proper response of real and reactive power commands, grid voltage support, and performance monitoring aligned with NGCP’s VRE (Variable Renewable Energy) testing standards.
